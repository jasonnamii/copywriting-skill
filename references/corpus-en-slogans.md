## 22구조 인덱스

### A. 명령형 (Imperative) — 영문 1순위
- A1 Just + Verb — "Just do it" (Nike)
- A2 Reverse Imperative — "Don't leave home without it" (AmEx)
- A3 Verb-First — "Think different" (Apple) / "Invent." (HP)

### B. 의문형
- B4 Direct Question — "Got Milk?" / "Where's the beef?"
- B5 Self-Mocking Q — "How many bars do you have?" (AT&T)

### C. 단정·선언
- C6 Brand=Result — "A diamond is forever" (De Beers)
- C7 Existential It's — "It's not TV. It's HBO."
- C8 Negation+Affirm — "Impossible is nothing" (Adidas)

### D. 대조·반전
- D9 Underdog — "We're number two. We try harder." (Avis)
- D10 The Other/Different — "Pork, the other white meat"
- D11 X without Y — "Power is nothing without control" (Pirelli)

### E. 약속·결과
- E12 Functional — "Once you pop, you can't stop" (Pringles)
- E13 Lifestyle — "We bring good things to life" (GE)
- E14 Emotional — "Because you're worth it" (L'Oreal)

### F. 운율·언어
- F15 Rhyme — "Beanz Meanz Heinz"
- F16 Pun — "More bars in more places" (Cingular)
- F17 Triplet — "Grace…Space…Pace" (Jaguar)
- F18 Alliteration — "Sense and Simplicity" (Philips)

### G. 슈퍼레이티브
- G19 World/America's #1 — "The world's local bank" (HSBC)
- G20 Ultimate/Only — "The ultimate driving machine" (BMW)

### H. 미니 스토리
- H21 If/When-Then — "When it absolutely, positively has to be there overnight" (FedEx)
- H22 Place Story — "What happens here stays here" (Las Vegas)

## 영문 카피 통계 (코퍼스 직관)

- **2~5단어 = 70%+** (Just do it 3단어, A diamond is forever 4단어)
- **마침표 1개**로 단정 호흡
- **모음 시작** (A/I/O) 브랜드 다수 (Apple/AT&T/Avis/Audi/AMD)
