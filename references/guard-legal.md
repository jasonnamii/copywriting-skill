# Guard: Legal Constraints by Copy Type

**Core Principle:** Legal requirements vary dramatically by market, platform, and copy type. Non-compliance triggers removal, fines, or brand damage. This guard maps requirements to copy decisions before writing.

---

## Korean Legal Requirements

### 1. 전자상거래법 (E-Commerce Law)

**Applies to:** Product copy, pricing, CTAs that initiate transaction

| Requirement | What It Means | Copy Impact |
|-------------|--------------|------------|
| **Price Display** | Must show final price (all taxes/fees included) | No "from $X" without total disclosure. No hidden fees in CTA copy |
| **Refund Policy** | Must state refund terms clearly (duration, conditions) | Cannot say "No refunds" in copy. Must link to explicit policy |
| **Cancellation Rights** | 14 days for cancellation | Cannot hide cancellation window. "Easy cancel anytime" requires legal evidence |
| **Seller Info** | Must show business name, address, contact | Footer/legal page required. Not copywriter's direct responsibility but copy can't obscure it |
| **Terms Visibility** | T&Cs must be one click away, readable | Cannot bury terms. "By signing up you agree to..." is OK if link is visible |

**Safe Harbor Language (Korean):**
```
이용약관 및 개인정보보호정책에 동의합니다
(I agree to Terms & Privacy Policy)

14일 이내 환불 가능하며, 취소는 언제든 가능해요
(Refund within 14 days; cancel anytime)
```

### 2. 표시광고법 (Advertising Standards Act)

**Applies to:** All marketing copy, headlines, claims, social posts, email subject lines

| Requirement | What It Means | Copy Impact |
|-------------|--------------|------------|
| **Substantiation** | All claims must be provable (특정 근거) | Cannot say "fastest" without comparative data. "Most users love it" needs survey proof |
| **Comparison Ads** | If you name competitor, must be factually accurate | Cannot say "better than X" unless documented. Better: claim own benefit without naming |
| **Endorsements** | Must disclose paid relationships (#AD, 광고) | Cannot disguise brand content as user review. Creator partnerships need clear disclosure |
| **Unsubstantiated Claims** | No "FDA approved," "clinically proven," "scientifically proven" without evidence | Avoid superlatives: "best," "only," "guaranteed" need proof |
| **Misleading Omissions** | Cannot omit material facts | If free trial converts to paid, must disclose in CTA copy |
| **Visual Deception** | Ads must accurately represent product | No misleading screenshots, filters, or heavily edited demos |

**Anti-Patterns:**
- "한국 No.1" without market data
- "과학적으로 증명됨" without study link
- "모든 사람이 사랑해요" without survey proof

**Safe Harbor Language:**
```
✓ "디자인 시간을 50% 단축한 사용자도 있어요" (data exists)
✓ "우리 고객 88%가 추천했어요" (survey evidence)
❌ "가장 빠른 디자인 도구" (unsubstantiated superlative)
```

---

### 3. 개인정보보호법 (Personal Information Protection Act)

**Applies to:** Signup flows, email copy, data collection CTAs, consent language

| Requirement | What It Means | Copy Impact |
|-------------|--------------|------------|
| **Affirmative Consent** | Cannot default to opt-in. Must be explicit agreement | Copy cannot hide consent. Checkbox must have clear label, not buried text |
| **Privacy Notice** | Must link to privacy policy at point of collection | "Sign up" CTA must mention data handling, or link to privacy page within 3 clicks |
| **Purpose Limitation** | Can only use data for stated purpose | Email signup copy can't say "marketing emails" if privacy policy says "transactional only" |
| **Right to Withdraw** | User can opt-out anytime | Every marketing email must have unsubscribe link. Copy cannot discourage it |
| **Sensitive Data** | Extra protection for health, finance, location, etc. | Cannot use "we keep your health data" casually. Must emphasize encryption, security |

**Safe Harbor Language:**
```
개인정보보호정책에 따라 이메일 뉴스레터를 받으실 수 있습니다.
(You may receive email newsletters per our Privacy Policy)

언제든 수신거부할 수 있어요 (모든 마케팅 이메일 하단에 필수)
(You can unsubscribe anytime — required in all marketing emails)
```

---

### 4. 앱스토어 심의 (App Store Review Guidelines)

**Applies to:** Mobile app copy, screenshots, in-app messaging, App Store description

| Requirement | What It Means | Copy Impact |
|-------------|--------------|------------|
| **Apple Review** | Copy must not make false health/safety claims | Cannot claim "cure," "treat," "prevent" disease. Can claim "help track," "support wellness" |
| **Spam Prevention** | No misleading CTAs (fake "close" buttons, dark patterns) | CTA copy must be honest about what happens. "Try free" cannot lead to surprise charge |
| **No Prohibited Content** | Hate speech, violence, illegal activity | Review all copy for offensive language, slurs, illegal services |
| **Pricing Transparency** | In-app purchase copy must show tier, duration, price | Cannot hide subscription details. "Subscribe now $9.99/month" clear in copy |
| **Keyword Stuffing** | App description cannot abuse search keywords | Copy should read naturally, not "fast fast fast app app app" |

**Common Rejects:**
- "Scientifically proven to improve health" (make it "supports wellness tracking")
- "Pro version (LIMITED TIME)" then always limited (must be genuinely limited)
- Misleading screenshots (add watermark "Representative image")

## English/Global Legal Requirements

### 1. FTC Endorsement Guidelines (US)

**Applies to:** Influencer partnerships, testimonials, affiliate marketing, social proof claims

| Requirement | What It Means | Copy Impact |
|-------------|--------------|------------|
| **Clear Disclosure** | #AD, #Sponsored, "Paid partnership" required | Cannot disguise paid promotion as organic endorsement |
| **Endorser Belief** | Endorser must genuinely believe the claim | Cannot pay someone to say something they don't believe |
| **Typical Results** | Claims must represent typical user experience | Cannot say "Save 10 hours" if only 1% of users see that benefit |
| **Material Connection** | Must disclose financial/personal relationship | If you have a code (save 20%), must disclose partnership in post caption |

**Safe Harbor Language:**
```
✓ "I was paid to promote this app, but I genuinely use it daily" + #AD
✓ "Get 20% off with code JASON — I'm a partner" + disclosure
❌ "My favorite app!" (hiding that you're paid)
```

---

### 2. GDPR Consent Language (EU/EEA)

**Applies to:** European user acquisition, email signup, ad targeting consent

| Requirement | What It Means | Copy Impact |
|-------------|--------------|------------|
| **Granular Consent** | Cannot ask "Do you want to use our service?" to collect all data | Must offer separate checkboxes: analytics, marketing, profiling |
| **Easy Withdrawal** | Unsubscribe link must be as easy to access as subscribe | Cannot require 5 clicks to unsubscribe. One-click preferred |
| **Privacy by Default** | Opt-out is not enough. Must opt-in explicitly | Copy cannot say "Uncheck if you don't want emails." Must be "Check if you want emails" |
| **Transparency** | Explain what data you collect and why | Copy must link to transparent privacy documentation, not legalese |

**Safe Harbor Language:**
```
☐ I'd like to receive marketing emails (checked = opt-in)
☐ I'd like to receive product updates (separate consent)

You can withdraw consent anytime by clicking unsubscribe in any email.
```

---

### 3. CAN-SPAM Requirements (Email, US/Canada)

**Applies to:** All marketing emails, newsletters, promotional messages

| Requirement | What It Means | Copy Impact |
|-------------|--------------|------------|
| **Honest Subject Line** | Subject cannot be misleading | Cannot say "Your receipt" if it's a promotional email. "Special offer: Save 20%" is clear |
| **Sender Identity** | Must clearly identify company | From line must show real business name, not "No-reply" or fake person |
| **Unsubscribe Option** | Must include working unsubscribe link | Cannot say "Reply to unsubscribe." Must be one-click link |
| **Physical Address** | Must include street address or PO box | Can be in footer, not in headline copy area |
| **Honor Opt-Out** | Remove person from list within 10 days | Copy cannot discourage unsubscribe ("You'll miss out!") |

**Red Flags:**
- Subject: "Quick question" (looks like personal email, misleading)
- "This is a one-time message" in marketing email (misleading)
- No unsubscribe visible (legal violation)

---

### 4. Platform Ad Policies

**Meta (Facebook/Instagram):**
- No before/after photos without medical evidence
- No "limited time" if not genuinely limited
- No misleading health claims ("lose weight in 7 days")
- No payday loans, cryptocurrency without restrictions

**Google Ads:**
- Restricted categories: Financial, health, legal (pre-approval required)
- No misleading destination (copy promises "free trial," landing page doesn't say free)
- No click bait ("Doctors HATE this one trick")

**TikTok:**
- No misleading product placement
- No health claims without substantiation
- No targeting minors with restricted products

## Copy Type × Legal Constraint Matrix

| Copy Type | E-Commerce Law | Ad Standards | Privacy | App Store | FTC | GDPR | CAN-SPAM | Platform Policy |
|-----------|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **A (UI/UX)** | High (if payment UX) | Medium | High (consent flow) | High (if mobile app) | Low | Medium (if EU) | N/A | Medium |
| **B (Strategy/Messaging)** | Low | High (claims substantiation) | Low | N/A | Low | Low | N/A | Low |
| **C (Campaign/Social)** | Medium (if e-commerce campaign) | **HIGH** | Medium | N/A | **HIGH** | Medium (if EU) | High (email C) | **HIGH** |
| **D (Email/Nurture)** | Low | Medium | High | N/A | Medium (if affiliate) | High (if EU) | **HIGH** | N/A |

**Decision Rule:** If constraint is HIGH, legal review required before shipping. If MEDIUM, self-audit checklist. If LOW, standard QC sufficient.

## Safe Harbor Language Templates

### Korean Templates

**Generic Consent (Privacy):**
```
개인정보보호정책에 동의하고 뉴스레터를 받으시겠어요?
(Do you agree to Privacy Policy and receive newsletters?)
```

**Affiliate/Partnership Disclosure:**
```
[광고] 다음 링크를 사용하면 제가 소액 수수료를 받습니다
([Ad] Using this link gives me a small commission)
```

**Substantiation Caveat:**
```
개인차가 있을 수 있습니다
(Results may vary based on individual circumstances)
```

**Refund Policy Minimum:**
```
구매 후 14일 이내 환불 가능합니다
(Full refund available within 14 days of purchase)
```

### English Templates

**FTC Disclosure (Influencer):**
```
#Ad - I was paid to promote this product.
```

**GDPR Consent (Email):**
```
☐ Yes, send me marketing updates
(You can unsubscribe anytime)
```

**CAN-SPAM Unsubscribe:**
```
Unsubscribe | Manage Preferences
```

**Platform Policy Compliance (Ad Copy):**
```
Results vary. See case studies for details.
Not medical advice. Consult a doctor.
```

## Legal Review Checklist by Type

### A Type (UI Copy) Checklist
- [ ] Any price displayed? All taxes/fees included or clearly noted?
- [ ] Any claims (speed, efficacy)? Do we have data?
- [ ] Signup flow? Privacy link visible?
- [ ] Mobile app? Read latest App Store guidelines
- [ ] Error messages? No misleading instructions?
- [ ] Buttons clear? "Next," "Confirm," "Subscribe" unambiguous?

### B Type (Strategy/Brand) Checklist
- [ ] Company values claim? Documented/provable?
- [ ] Competitive claim ("fastest," "best")? Data available?
- [ ] Public statement about data/security? Legal approved?
- [ ] Mission statement claim sustainability? Verifiable?
- [ ] Any "guarantee"? Defined terms?

### C Type (Campaign) Checklist
- [ ] Headline makes a claim? Substantiation available?
- [ ] Social post? Hashtag #Ad if sponsored/affiliate?
- [ ] Influencer collaboration? Creator disclosure included?
- [ ] Image/demo accurate representation? Not heavily filtered?
- [ ] CTA leads where it says? No surprises?
- [ ] "Limited time"? Actually limited, with end date?
- [ ] Platform policy read? No banned categories?
- [ ] Before/after? Medical/health product? Pre-approval obtained?

### D Type (Email/Nurture) Checklist
- [ ] Subject line honest? Not misleading?
- [ ] From line shows real company name?
- [ ] Unsubscribe link visible and working?
- [ ] Sender address/phone in footer?
- [ ] Removed recent unsubscribes?
- [ ] Email list consented? (opt-in, not opt-out)
- [ ] If GDPR audience: granular consent stored?
- [ ] Affiliate link disclosed if applicable?

## Pre-Launch Legal Workflow

**Day -3:** Share copy draft with legal team
**Day -2:** Legal review + feedback (substantiation needed? disclosure required?)
**Day -1:** Revisions incorporated + final legal sign-off
**Day 0:** Ship

**If no legal team:** Use Safe Harbor templates (conservative approach, lower risk)

## Common Violations & Fixes

| Violation | Risk | Copy Example | Fix |
|-----------|------|-------------|-----|
| Unsubstantiated superlative | FTC enforcement, platform ban | "The world's fastest design tool" | "Designed for speed" or show benchmark data |
| Hidden subscription terms | FTC lawsuit, chargeback surge | "Start free" (then surprise charge) | "14-day free trial, then $99/month" |
| Misleading subject line | CAN-SPAM violation, ISP block | "You've won a prize" (promotional email) | "Exclusive offer: Save $50 today" |
| No affiliate disclosure | FTC fine up to $43,000 | "I love this app!" (paid endorsement) | "I'm a partner (get 20% off): [link]" |
| App Store health claims | App removal | "Cures sleep issues" | "Tracks sleep patterns, helping you rest better" |
| No GDPR unsubscribe | Up to €20,000 fine | Unsubscribe requires form submission | "Click here to unsubscribe instantly" |

## Final Checkpoint

Before shipping ANY copy:

1. **Type Identification:** Is this A/B/C/D?
2. **Market Check:** Is audience Korean? EU? US? Global mix?
3. **Constraint Mapping:** Use matrix above. What constraints apply (HIGH priority)?
4. **Substantiation:** Do I have proof for every claim?
5. **Disclosure:** Is this affiliate/partnership/endorsement? Disclosed?
6. **Platform Check:** Does this violate Meta/Google/Apple/TikTok policy?
7. **Legal Template:** Using safe harbor language where applicable?
8. **Sign-Off:** If HIGH constraint exists, legal reviewed before ship.

If any fails → return to guard checklist, fix, then ship.
